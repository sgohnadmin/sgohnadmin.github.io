About Me

Tell Me a Little Bit About Yourself?

I began my formal studies at Penn State University. There, I majored in Information Sciences and Technology. My first technical job was programming direct mail. From that experience, I transitioned into variable data templates and web-to-print technologies. Later, my role expanded further, including branding landing pages, creating website themes, and adding site functionalities. I also had the opportunity to lead site administration and support. Most recently, I helped guide the implementation of site refreshing and improvements. This led to navigating client pain points and developing solutions to ensure retention. Throughout my career, I have continued to educate and learn, some company-provided and some driven by my curiosity to remain knowledgeable of emerging web technologies.
